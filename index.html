Advertising Best Practices

CPM (Cost Per Mille)

CPM is the cost per thousand ad impressions. It's best used for brand awareness campaigns where the goal is to maximize exposure.

CPC (Cost Per Click)

CPC is the amount you pay each time a user clicks on your ad. It's crucial to optimize ad copy and landing pages to achieve a lower CPC and higher conversion rate.

ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)

ROAS measures the revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising. A successful campaign should aim for a ROAS that reflects a profitable margin.
